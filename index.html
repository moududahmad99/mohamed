Real World Impact Numbers

1. Chronic Disease Management

  • Diabetes: ↓1.5–2% HbA1c (vs. usual care) through medication adjustments and adherence support (JAPhA)
  • ↓35% risk of complications (e.g., neuropathy, retinopathy) with MTM interventions (Annals of Pharmacotherapy)
  • Hypertension: ↑25% more patients achieve blood pressure control with pharmacist-led MTM (JAMA)
  • ↓$1,200/year per patient in cardiovascular-related costs (AJHP)

2. Hospitalizations & Costs

  • ↓30% hospital readmissions for heart failure patients post-discharge (New England Journal of Medicine)
  • ↓50% adverse drug events (ADEs) in elderly patients through medication reconciliation (Archives of Internal Medicine)
  • 4–12 ROI per $1 spent on MTM due to avoided ER visits/hospitalizations (CMS/PQA data)

3. Mental Health & High-Risk Populations

  • ↑40% adherence to antidepressants with pharmacist follow-ups (Psychiatric Services)
  • ↓20% opioid misuse in pain management patients via MTM monitoring (JAPhA)

4. Public Health Impact

  • MTM programs in Medicare Part D (2021 data):
  • 8.4 million patients served annually
  • $1.2 billion saved in avoidable hospitalization costs
